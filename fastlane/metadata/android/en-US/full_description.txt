RUTEdu is an interactive educational app for high school students, developed at PRZ (Rzeszów University of Technology).

Features:
* Interactive periodic table of all 118 elements
* Chemistry quizzes with automatic question generator
* Chemical equation balancing with number keypad
* Geography quiz with interactive map (click-a-country)
* Local multiplayer mode over WiFi (1v1 and tournament)
* Progress tracking per player profile

No ads, no tracking, no internet required (except map quiz).
